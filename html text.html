        Plagiarism and Referencing Check

        Blackboard provides a coursework originality checking tool called SafeAssign which helps identify plagiarism.

        You can use the Plagiarism and Referencing Check course for the purposes of checking your work through SafeAssign, in advance of you making your real coursework submission via the Blackboard Coursework tab. Once in the course, click the 'Enrol' button.

        Guidance on using SafeAssign is available within the course and on the Blackboard support website.

        Information Security Training

        The Information Security Training module will give you a solid understanding of basic security principles when handling information – from an individual's legal responsibilities through to identifying potentially malicious emails.  By the end of this course you will be able to:

        •    Choose more secure passwords
        •    Understand how malware tries to infect your computer
        •    Identify email phishing attacks
        •    Learn how to securely handle confidential information”
